The organising group is made up of Streatham residents who came together over social media in response to the Covid-19 outbreak. It includes a emeritus Professor of Computer Science from Imperial College, a civil servant acting in a personal capacity, a retired LSE IT training specialist and charity trustee, a litigation solicitor at law firm Leigh Day (acting in a personal capacity)

We are supported by InStreatham - the Business Improvement District organisation for Streatham, the MP for Streatham, Bell Riberio-Addy, and Streatham Space Project. We are keen to broaden our support and work with other organisations who support our goals too. Please email: covidtechaid@gmail.com
