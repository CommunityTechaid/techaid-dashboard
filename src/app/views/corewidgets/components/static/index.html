Lambeth Covid TechAid

At the moment, there are hundreds of people stuck at home without a suitable device to access the internet – to communicate with loved ones, to download educational resources, or to even get some basic entertainment. It's our aim to collect your unwanted devices and deliver them to people who really need them, to help combat isolation and boredom.
